--- title: "Should Perspective be Shared: Journalists, Opinion and Social Media" collection: talks type: "Paper Presentation" permalink: /talks/APPE-2020.html venue: "Association for Practical and Professional Ethics" date: 2020 location: "Atlanta GA, USA" --- No reporter can ever claim to be entirely objective in their craft, but the presence of social media use promulgates these individual perspectives to a wider audience than ever before, changing the question from “how opinionated should reporters be in their work” to “should reporters share their opinions at all, on any medium?” [Paper](lukedanielcross.github.io/files/shouldperspectivebeshared.pdf) [Powerpoint] (lukedanielcross.github.io/files/shouldperspectivebeshared.ppt)
