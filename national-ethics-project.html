The National Ethics Project (NEP) is a consortium of researchers, educators, and practitioners from different disciplines and a variety of higher ed institutions. My role is managing the NEP's web presence and heading a small team of communications specialist's, overseeing and contributing to the reporting behind our Spotlight on Ethics series, where we highlight innovative ethics education across the country.
